Suggested Learning Path

A recommended order for IT pros starting from zero AI knowledge and building toward agent development.

01
AI Literacy
Start with Elements of AI or Google AI Essentials to understand what AI is and what it can do.
02
Prompt Engineering
Learn to write effective prompts with DeepLearning.AI or AWS Foundations of Prompt Engineering.
03
AI for IT Support
Apply AI to your daily work with ChatGPT Tips for the Help Desk and IBM AI Fundamentals.
04
Build AI Agents
Create your first agents with Hugging Face Agents Course, then explore LangGraph and CrewAI.
